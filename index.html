How your Dubai setup journey works

A clear, four-step process with defined timelines. We keep you informed at every stage, and you can choose to add our paid bank account opening support at the right moment.

Discovery and structure call

We review your jurisdiction, business model, shareholders and banking needs. You receive a recommended structure, cost overview and outline of optional bank account support.

Document preparation and filings

We prepare applications, coordinate notarisation and submit documents to the selected authority or Free Zone. You receive a simple checklist of what we need from you.

Licence issuance & bank support

After approval, you can choose our paid bank account opening support. We prepare bank files, brief you for KYC, and coordinate with relationship managers to ensure your case is understood clearly.

Visas, tax & ongoing support

We assist with UAE residency visas, corporate tax registration and help connect you to bookkeeping, audit and legal partners whenever required.

Frequently asked questions
How long does it really take to set up my company? +
For straight forward cases, Free Zone setups usually take 1 to 2 weeks after we receive complete documents. Mainland LLC setups may take slightly longer, depending on activity and approvals.
Can you guarantee a bank account? +
No. Bank approval always rests with the bank. Our paid bank account opening support improves how your case is presented and how well you are prepared, but we never guarantee approval.
Is bank account opening included in your base fees? +
No. Structuring, licensing and core advisory are included in our base fees. Bank account opening support is a separate optional service with its own clearly quoted fee.
Do I need to be physically present in Dubai? +
Some Free Zones allow remote setup, but banks and immigration often require at least one visit. We plan your trip so you can complete key steps in a focused window.
How are your fees structured? +
We use transparent, fixed fees that combine government costs and our advisory work. Any paid add-ons, such as bank account support, are listed separately for clarity.
Can you coordinate tax and legal advice? +
Yes. Where required, we bring in UAE tax, legal and audit partners and align the Dubai structure with your global position or existing advisers.
